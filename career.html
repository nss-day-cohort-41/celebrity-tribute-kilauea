Career Intro

Dean Martin started his music career at the age of 17. Two years later Martin went on his first tour as a lead vocalist. By 1946, Martin had a 15 minitue radio special and had several songs recorded with Diamond records.

By 1950, Martin teamed up with Jerry Lewis and obtained a NBC radio special. The duo would go on to make 16 movies together.

He began the Dean Martin Show in 1965. Once he started performing in Las Vegas with his famous friends they became known as the Rat Pack. Martin would go on to make over 50 movies.

Skills

Dean Martin was an actor; who stared in over 60 films with famous legends such as John Wayne. NBC's The Dean Martin Show ended up running for 8 years.

Additonally, Dean Martin was a world renown singer with over 30 studio albumns across several genres of music, and lastly in the early days of Dean Martin's career NBC gave him and Jerry Lewis their own radio show.
